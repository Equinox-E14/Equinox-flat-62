A note from the vendor

Twenty years.
One remarkable home.

I have often thought that this home gave me something increasingly rare in London: room to breathe.

Over twenty years, the river, the open sky, and the changing light around Island Gardens gradually shaped the rhythm of daily life here. Morning coffee outside, walks beside the Thames, and the ease of stepping into Greenwich whenever the mood suited became rituals, not just activities.

This became far more than simply a flat; it has been my sanctuary within the city. The true luxury here isn't something you can easily measure in a standard estate agent layout. It is the daily feeling of total peace it provides.

Leaving for the south coast now feels like the right next chapter, but the quiet rhythm of this place will be missed enormously.

I vividly remember the day I first saw the terrace. It was one of those glorious, sunny and warm April days we sometimes get.
